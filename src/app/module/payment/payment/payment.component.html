DooyD Payment Policy

1. Buyer will make payment to dooyd.com upon place an order.

2. Dooyd.com will send notification to ship the item once the payment is received by dooyd.com. Seller will start ship the item only after order full amount is received by dooyd.com.

3. Seller will receive the payments after receive the items by buyer. Payment will be made to seller within 2 days once confirm the receipt of the products by buyer.
